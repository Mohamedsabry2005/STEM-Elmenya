Welcome in STEM Elmenya

Overview

An excellent education is offered at STEM Elmenya, a public high school in Egypt, to pupils who are driven by academics.

STEM schools cater to high-potential kids who have excelled in the classroom and offer a distinctive system that concentrates on the scientific, mathematical, and technological sectors.

ِAbout us.

mission

The goal of STEM Elmenya High School for students is to give pupils a rigorous math, scientific, and technology-focused learning environment, to enthuse about the possibility of discovery, and to develop an innovative culture based on moral conduct and the common good of humanity.

vision

We at STEM Elmenya High School think the institution was intended to produce "centres of excellence" that represent cross-disciplinary cooperation, practical application, critical thinking, and a spirit of innovation. In the end, STEM institutions foster a new perception of Egypt as a nation having the institutional and human resources necessary to compete and succeed in the global economy. In order to educate high-achieving students from across Egypt, regardless of socioeconomic status, the schools' main objective is to establish a network of self-sufficient schools with newly established curricula, qualified teachers, industry partnerships, and an operational system under the MOE's public schooling system.

Admission.

The following two requirements must be satisfied by applicants in order to be accepted into STEM Elmenya:

having a mark of 100% in one of the three subjects—math, science, or English—and a cumulative grade of more than 98% in the ninth grade.

having a grade of 100% in two out of the three subjects—math, science, and English—and a cumulative grade of more than 95% in the ninth grade.
